Teach Your Kids How To Type with Type Tap

Fun, Easy and Interactive: Type Tap teaches children how to type using letter dictation and enriches their vocabulary by connecting each word to a corresponding image. Each successful tap is rewarded with a whimsical sound that transforms play time into an engaging learning experience. Type Tap is designed for children ages 3 and up and contains hundreds of words from a variety of different categories.

Tap, Tap, Type!

Type Tap combines the exciting sights and sounds of gameplay with interactive language exercises to create an immersive learning experience. Who knew discovering new words could be so much fun!

Type by Category

Teaching your children how to type is not a task many parents want to take on (you’ve got enough on your plate). Plus with so many words to tackle, it can be hard to know where to start. That’s why Type Tap lessons are organized into fun categories so you can select the subject that is most engaging for your child and immerse them in a learning activity tailored to their needs. We call that #ParentingWin.

Type and Talk

Type Tap’s best aspect is the full learning potential it provides kids.

While typing can be a fun way for kids to pass the time and learn letters - it's time to level up. Push the boundaries of their learning capabilities by having them actually retain the meaning of every word they type out. How? Through consistent letter-by-letter pronunciation and final word dictation that’ll encourage imitation practices. Your child will seamlessly learn the meaning of each word through its association with its paired image.

Fast, easy and natural learning.

Track your Child's Progress

Parents should never be left out of their kids' learning journey- that’s why we made sure to let you in on the fun by giving you immediate, ongoing feedback after each ‘lesson’, allowing you to track your kids’ progress (and be proud of their successes). For each category you’ll be able to see a summary of your kids’ progress, monitoring the areas they mastered and those they still need to improve in.

Any Device, Any Language, Anytime.

Kids are always on the move, and their classes should be too. Type Tap works on multiple devices, from iPhones to Androids and iPads to Desktops, there’s no limit on how, when and where to learn. Want to get your kid interested in another language? With Type Tap they can explore more than just their mother tongue, giving them a head start to the school year and...well, life.
