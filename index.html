Teach Your Kids How To Type with Tappy Tap

Fun, Easy and Interactive - Tappy tap teaches kids to type with letter dictation and enriches their vocabulary by tying words to corresponding images. Tappy Tap is designed for children ages 3 and up and contains hundreds of words from a variety of different categories.

Tap, Tap, Type!

Ever thought of putting ‘learning’, ’fun’ and ‘addictive’ in the same sentence? Well now you can. Discovering and learning different words with Tappy tap gives your child the best of both worlds with bite-sized classes filled with fun sights and sounds that’ll keep your kids immersed in the learning experience.

Type by Category

Keeping your kids focused can be challenging enough, let alone teaching them a bunch of new words and how to type them. That’s why we’ve adapted the lessons into fun categories, tailoring them to your little one’s individual needs to keep them motivated.

Type, See, Remember

Tappy Tap’s best aspect is the full learning potential it provides kids. While typing can be a fun way for kids to pass the time and learn letters - it's time to level up. Push the boundaries of their learning capabilities by having them actually retain the meaning of every word they type out. How? Through consistent letter-by-letter pronunciation and final word dictation that’ll encourage imitation practices. Your child will seamlessly learn the meaning of each word through its association with its paired image. Fast, easy and natural learning.

Track your Child's Progress

Parents should never be left out of their kids' learning journey - that’s why we made sure to let you in on the fun by giving you immediate, ongoing feedback after each ‘lesson’, allowing you to track your kids’ progress (and be proud of their successes). For each category you’ll be able to see a summary of your kids’ progress, monitoring the areas they mastered and those they still need to improve in.

Any Device, Any Language, Anytime.

Kids are always on the move, and their classes should be too. Tappy Tap works on multiple devices, from iPhones to Androids and iPads to Desktops, there’s no limit on how, when and where to learn. Want to get your kid interested in another language? With Tappy Tap they can explore more than just their mother tongue, giving them a head start to the school year and...well, life.
